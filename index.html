Game of War

Rules

  • Each player gets 26 cards
  • Each player turns up a card at the same time and the player with the higher card wins both cards
  • If the cards are the same rank, it is War. Three cards will automatically be drawn from each pile and the fourth card is turned over. The player with the higher card takes all 8 cards
  • The game ends when one player runs out of cards in their pile. The player with all the cards is declared the winner.
